Overview of the service

Service description

Allows the general public to get a polymerase chain reaction (PCR) test to check whether they have COVID-19. They can book a test, find an appropriate way to take the test (either by traveling to a test centre, or taking it at home) and then have the test analysed in a lab and be sent the results.

The service as experienced by users

This information has been provided by the service team.
Steps Delivered by
1. Realises they have COVID symptoms Information on GOV.UK, NHS.UK describing cardinal symptoms and directing people to get a PCR test if they have them.
2. Books a test This can be done via the online service or by phone on 119
3. Takes a test This can be done either at home or through various testing sites including drive-through, walk-through etc.
4. Recieves result Results are sent to people via text messages and emails.
5. Shares contacts See Share contacts services.
6. Self-isolates See Self-isolation services.
